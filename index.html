About Me

Hi! My name is Julianne and I am a Colorado native living near Denver. I live with my partner, our two 7-year-old boys, and two dogs. Before I started coding I worked as a special education instructor with young adults ages 18-21, teaching them job skills, life skills, and social/coping skills to navigate their adult lives independently. I have always loved working with technology and was lucky enough to be sent to a coding workshop for professional development, and I loved it! I began my journey into coding after that, and can't wait to keep growing my skills and knowledge in the field!
